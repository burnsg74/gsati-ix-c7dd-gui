Click the three dots in lower left → Access (Menu) → Web Behavior Profiles (Tab) → New Profile (Button).
Enter your front end website domain(s) for tracking

  • Status: Enabled
  • Track Abandoned Carts: Enabled
  • Enable guest carts: Disabled
